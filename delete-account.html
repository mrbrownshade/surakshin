Delete your account & data

For the Surakshin: Learn Cybersecurity app, by Brownshade Studio · Updated 3 July 2026

You can permanently delete your Surakshin account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do it — instantly inside the app, or by email if you no longer have the app installed.

Option 1 — In the app (instant)

  1. Open Surakshin and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Tap the settings (gear) icon in the top-right.
  3. Under Your Data, tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and data are erased immediately.

Option 2 — By email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address associated with your account:

  • Email: brownshadestudio@gmail.com
  • Subject: Delete my Surakshin account

We verify the request and delete your account and data within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account (authentication identity) and display name.
  • All learning data — XP, level, rank, streaks, badges, and lesson/lab progress.
  • Any account record held in our systems.

What may be retained

Anonymised, aggregated analytics and crash statistics that cannot be linked back to you or your account may be retained for a limited period per our providers' defaults. We do not keep any data that identifies you after deletion, and we never sell your data.
