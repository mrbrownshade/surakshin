Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026 · Effective on first release

This Privacy Policy explains how Brownshade Studio ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Surakshin: Learn Cybersecurity mobile application (the "App"). Surakshin is an educational app. All security labs inside it are fully simulated and never interact with real networks, devices, or systems.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information — if you create an account, your email address and display name (via Firebase Authentication, including optional Google sign-in). You may also use the App anonymously.
  • Profile & learning data — your chosen display name, XP, level, rank, streaks, badges, and lesson/lab progress.

Information collected automatically

  • Diagnostics — crash reports and performance data (Firebase Crashlytics) to keep the App stable. On by default; you can turn this off any time in Settings → Share anonymous analytics.
  • Analytics — anonymised usage events (e.g. a lesson or lab completed) to understand what to improve (Google Analytics for Firebase). On by default; the same Settings switch turns it off.
  • Purchase records — if you subscribe to Surakshin Pro, Google Play processes the payment and provides us a purchase token used to verify your subscription. We never receive or store your card or payment details.
  • Device integrity — Google Play Integrity / Firebase App Check tokens used only to protect our backend from abuse.

We do not collect the Advertising ID, and we do not use your data for advertising or attribution. We do not collect your contacts, precise location, photos, microphone, camera, SMS, or call data. We do not sell your personal data.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and sync your learning progress across sessions and devices.
  • To operate gamification features (XP, streaks, ranks, leaderboards).
  • To process and validate subscription purchases.
  • To keep the App secure, understand usage, and diagnose crashes (you can opt out in Settings).
  • To send optional streak/learning reminders (only if you enable notifications).

3. Legal bases (GDPR / India DPDP / applicable law)

We process data to perform our contract with you (providing the App) and on the basis of our legitimate interest in keeping the App secure and improving it. Analytics and crash reporting are enabled by default but are entirely optional — you can withdraw at any time via Settings → Share anonymous analytics, and we honour that choice immediately. We do not process data for advertising.

4. Third-party services

We use the following providers, each under their own privacy terms:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Remote Config, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging, App Check, Crashlytics, Analytics) — Firebase Privacy
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing for subscriptions — Google Privacy

5. Data retention

We keep your account and learning data until you delete your account. Analytics and diagnostic data are retained for a limited period per the provider's defaults and then aggregated or deleted.

6. Your rights & choices

  • Delete your account — In the App, go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently erases your profile, progress, and associated data from our systems.
  • Export your data — Request a copy of your data from within the App's Settings.
  • Access, correction, objection — Contact us using the details below.
  • Opt out of analytics — Turn off analytics, diagnostics, or notifications in Settings at any time.

7. Children's privacy

Surakshin is rated Teen and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Data security

  • All network traffic is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3).
  • Sensitive local data is stored using the device's secure storage; no secrets are embedded in the App.
  • Backend access is governed by least-privilege security rules and protected by App Check.
  • Subscription entitlements are verified server-side; payment card data is never handled by us.

9. International transfers

Our providers may process data on servers outside your country. Where required, appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) apply.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notified in the App.
